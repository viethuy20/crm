QUESTIONS MUST BE ANSWERED BEFORE LAUNCHING AN EVENT.


1. Am I sure the topic is positively received by market?

2. Am I certain the course agenda contains all details target audience needs?

3. Is the course brochure too basic ? or too advance?

4. What is not necessary in the course brochure ?

5. Is phone research sample good enough to represent the whole market ?

6. Is that the common budget of the market ?

7. Is that the common time that market prefer ?

8. Did I speak to the right person?

9. HR decides training budget and time for training, Have I speak to HR about event?
